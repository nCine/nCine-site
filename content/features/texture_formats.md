- Image formats: [PNG](http://www.libpng.org/pub/png/libpng.html), [WebP](https://developers.google.com/speed/webp/)
- GPU compressed formats: ASTC, ETC2, ATC, PVR, DXT
- Container formats: KTX, PVR, DDS
